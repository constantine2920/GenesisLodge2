ACTIVITIES

Genesis Lodge provides a funded activity program which encourages interaction, independence and development of friendships. The goal of the program is to promote a sense of self worth as individuals feel part of a family and the community around them. The program also assists individuals to build social skills and life skills by involvement in community programs such as Friends and Advocates, Hospital Day Treatment Programs and Rehabilitation Programs. The Activity Program also includes outings to entertainment, sporting venues, theatrical shows and restaurants.

At Genesis Lodge we provide a clean and safe surrounding which brings peace of mind to all.
